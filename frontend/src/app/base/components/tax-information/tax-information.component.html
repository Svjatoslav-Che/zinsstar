Tax information from Interest Union

We at Zinsunion provide you as a customer with tax-relevant information on overnight and fixed-term deposits at our partner banks free of charge. In the case of the Zinsunion Invest portfolios, tax treatment takes place at the level of the custodian bank DAB BNP Paribas - these are therefore not included in the tax information of Zinsunion.
