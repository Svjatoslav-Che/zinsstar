Cooperation with the bank comparison
Zinsunion cooperates with Bankenvergleich AG (hereinafter " Zinsunion Bank "), which manages the customer's current account (hereinafter " Zinsunion account "). The interest union account serves as a clearing account for payment orders between a reference account (usually the customer's house bank account) and the respective account of a partner bank. Zinsunion and Zinsunion Bank support the customer in concluding the deposit agreement and in communicating with the respective partner bank.

After registration, the customer sets a password for access to the platform and online banking. This is followed by the opening of the Interest Union account (including identification). As part of the range of investment products, a clearing account and a securities account (hereinafter referred to as “ custody account") Opened for the customer. The bank comparison transfers the desired investment amount to the account maintained for the customer's deposit at the partner bank or to the clearing account created for the settlement of orders placed. Via online banking, the customer can, depending on the conditions of the respective offer used, order extensions, early terminations and other transactions in connection with an offer and receive messages from partner banks and DAB (e.g. account statements).

Correspondence (e.g. contracts, account statements) from interest union, bank comparison and partner banks is stored individually and transparently for each customer in the electronic mailbox of online banking. In addition, a German-speaking customer service is available to support customers and answer questions.
